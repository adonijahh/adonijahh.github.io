Yutong Tian (Student Member, IEEE) received the B.S. degree in electronic information engineering from Southwest University, Chongqing, China, in 2021. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in electronic science and technology with the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), Chengdu, China.He was admitted to the China Association for Science and Technology Young Talent Support Project Doctoral Special Program in 2024. He was with the College of Sciences and Engineering, University of Tasmania, Hobart, TAS, Australia, as a Visiting Student, in 2018. His research interests include radar signal processing, digital beamforming, wireless communication, machine olfaction and machine learning.

田雨桐，电子科学与工程学院电子科学与技术专业2023级博士研究生，导师为黎湧教授、杨海宁研究员。研究方向为毫米波通信、雷达系统信号处理。目前以第一作者身份在IEEE TGRS, IEEE TIM, IEEE Sensors Journal等国际期刊上发表论文6篇，在IEEE AP-S, IEEE ICMMT等国内外顶尖会议上发表论文8篇，授权发明专利2项，曾获本科、硕士、博士国家奖学金、学术青苗奖、学术新秀奖等，入选中国科协青年人才托举工程博士生专项。曾参加“华为杯”中国研究生数学建模竞赛获二等奖。作为学生主研，参与多项纵向科研项目，面向国家低轨卫星互联网战略需求，参与搭建中国星网GW星座系统，提出的“低轨卫星星地信道特性建模与扩展建模”方法被中国星网采纳。现为IEEE Access、ICLR等多个期刊、会议审稿人。
